# Welcome to Lost
You wake up on a beach.
Saltwater burns in your mouth. Waves wash over your legs, and your head pounds as the memories slowly return. The plane. The screams. The aircraft breaking apart in the sky. The front section fell one way, the rear section crashed into the sea — and after that, only darkness.
Now you are here.
The only question is where here is. And whether anyone else survived.
Your first task is simple: stay alive. Gather wood, food, and water. Once you have enough, you can use your glasses and the sun to light a fire. The fire gives warmth, hope, and protection — but it must be fed with wood constantly.
The stronger the fire burns, the greater the chance that other survivors will find your camp. As your group grows, use the buttons on each person’s card to assign tasks: gather resources, rest, keep watch, build, or explore.
Over time, you may discover new ways to survive. Craft tools. Improve the camp. Venture deeper into the island. Perhaps there are clues from the crash. Perhaps there is a way home.
## Tips for your first run
Start on easy difficulty and low speed. Gather at least 7 wood, 2 food, and 2 water before lighting the fire. Make sure you are rested too. Time is of the essence, so do not let anyone fall into to much reflection. Once the fire is burning, focus on keeping it alive until the first survivor finds your camp. After that, the real struggle begins.
